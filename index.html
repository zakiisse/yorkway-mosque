Serving the community

Yorkway Mosque is a community hub that serves the Muslims and neighbours of the local area. Inspired by faith, the mosque is a place of worship and a place of welcome for young and old, Muslim and non-Muslim.

Through daily prayers, classes, youth activities and community programmes, we hope to nurture hearts and serve our wider society.
