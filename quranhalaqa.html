Weekly Quran Halaqa

“The best of you are the ones who learn the Quran and teach it.”
- Sahih al-Bukhari 5027

Read and Memorise the Quran

Join our weekly Quran Halaqa where we read 1 page a week either from memory or by reading to the Sheikh. Our sessions are designed for both beginners and advanced learners, providing an opportunity to learn recitation, tajweed, and memorization under the guidance of experienced Teachers. The Halaqa is completely free and open to all ages and abilities. With the tawfeeq of Allah, the Halaqa has been ongoing since 2004, and is still going strong to this day.

  • When: Every Friday starting at 7:30
  • Where: Yorkway Mosque, Main Hall
  • Who: Open to all ages and levels
  • Contact: +44 07482676779
